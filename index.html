About Us

Welcome to Torque X

Team Torque-X is a dynamic group of students from the Meenakshi sundararajan engineering college, chennai, tamilnadu, India. Operating under the inter disciplinary, the team specializes in the research, design, and development of off-road vehicles, including both combustion engine and electric models. Our focus is on creating high-performance, all-terrain vehicles tailored for competitions such as SAE BAJA, ATVC, and FMAE. Dedicated to innovation and excellence, Team Torque-X continually pushes the boundaries of engineering to excel in these rigorous competitions.
