ABOUT TORQUE-X

Our Achievements

Team Torque-X is proud to be recognized by Tata Motors through the SAE BAJA Innovation Event! Our innovation — an HCNG Gas Leakage Detection System — was designed to enhance the safety of hydrogen-enriched CNG (HCNG) vehicles by detecting and alerting users of any gas leaks in real time. This recognition by Tata Motors and the BAJA committee stands as a huge validation of our idea and engineering effort. Months of hard work, late-night problem-solving, and non-stop passion have driven us to this proud milestone — and this is only the beginning!

Vision

Our vision is to shape the future of sustainable mobility through innovative electric vehicle engineering. We aim to nurture the next generation of engineers by encouraging creativity, teamwork, and technical expertise. Our mission is to continuously improve our designs, enhance performance, and promote green technologies while representing our institution at the national level with pride and excellence.
